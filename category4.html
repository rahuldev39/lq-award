Eligibility

  • Nominations are open to all Law Firms of India in India or abroad.
  • The Law Firm should be registered under Indian Laws and recognized by the Bar Council of India.
  • The nominee should be an Indian citizen.
  • For the special category of nominees with less than 10 years’ experience, the date of incorporation of the Law Firm should be less than 10 years ago as on 31st October, 2020.

Assessment Criteria

Submissions will be judged on the basis of responses to the following questions along with the supporting and additional material.

Kindly answer each question in 500 words max:

  • How has the Firm demonstrated professional excellence in legal practice during the period 1st April, 2019 to 30th September, 2020?
  • What is the Firm’s business strategy and vision? How does it aim to achieve its long-term goals?
  • How has the Firm contributed to the growth and evolution of the legal profession in India?

Mandatory Information

Kindly provide the following details to enhance your submission:

  • Upload a high-resolution company logo.
  • Name of the organization and its size in terms of number of offices and employees.
  • In which year was the organization set up?
  • Kindly mention the current and past practice areas of law that the Firm specialises in.
  • Is the Firm currently a member of any law association(s)? If yes, then kindly specify the name(s).
  • Kindly list any external groups, associations, or bodies that the Firm is a member of.
  • Has the Firm done any pro-bono work? If yes, then kindly provide details.

Optional Supporting Material

You could provide the following supporting documents:

  • References, testimonials
  • Media coverage, statistics and data reports and/or financial or business metrics to support your nomination.
  • Submit an optional short video, up to 30 seconds long, in which you talk about “What Featuring in the Top List of Forbes means to me.”

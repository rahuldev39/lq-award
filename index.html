Awards Process

1
Apply

All applications should be sent via this online nomination portal by filling a form and uploading supporting documents.

The ‘Legal Powerlist 2020’ involves Six (6) categories and Forty (40) sub-categories/practice areas drawn from different sectors in India. Participants in Top 50 law firms and Top 100 Lawyers category can apply to a total of 5 (five) sub-categories/ practice areas (1+4) listed on the website with an additional cost in each sub-category.

After the nominations are received, the organisers will screen each one of them to ensure that the application process is complete and will contact the applicant in case any more information/mandatory documents are required.

Evaluation

Our esteemed Jury will first compile a preliminary shortlist of the most eligible candidates from the entire gamut of nominations

The esteemed Jury will then prepare a final list of candidates in each category and sub-category/practice area of the Awards.

This process may require the Jury members to do external research including communicating with the references provided by the nominees.

2
3
Shortlisting

The names of all shortlisted candidates will be announced in the first week of February. The finalists will be drawn from this shortlist by the Jury after further evaluation.

Felicitation

The Winners selected by the esteemed Jury will be announced on February 28, 2021 at an online event which will also be telecast by CNBC/CNN Network 18.

The Winners’ list will also be published in Forbes India magazine and on forbesindia.com, https://legal-powerlist.netlify.app and on LegitQuest portals legitquest.com and legiteye.com.
